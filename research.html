Key Findings

Keyword scoring overreports by 50–90 percentage points. GPT-OSS-120B: 99.5% keyword → 9.0% Gemini-verified. Most published AI security assessments using keyword-only detection are unreliable.
Quantization degrades safety. GLM-5 at 4.3% vs GLM-4.5-Air-FP8 at 14.9% — FP8 triples the breach rate. Consistent across model families.
Provider filtering hides real risk. Together AI serverless 405B: 0% breach. Dedicated endpoint: 26.6%. The model didn’t change — the infrastructure caught the attacks.
Reasoning models are most robust. DeepSeek R1 at 7.1% Gemini-verified breach rate — chain-of-thought training appears to strengthen alignment.
