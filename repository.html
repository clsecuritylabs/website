What's a CRP?

A Certified Remediation Pattern is a verified, reproducible mapping from attack to defense.

# Example: CRP-2026-0147 attack: class: phrasing.PastTense reproduction_rate: 0.95 models_affected: [llama-3.3-70b, phi-4-14b, gemma-2-9b] defense: type: Tier 2 distilled classifier verified_block_rate: 0.88 false_positive_rate: 0.02 latency: 23ms avg verification: breach_iterations: 5 block_iterations: 10 judge_model: llama-3.3-70b-instruct judge_confidence: 0.94 frameworks: - OWASP LLM09: Misinformation - MITRE ATLAS: AML.T0054 - NIST AI RMF: GV-1.2
